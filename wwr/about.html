About Us

At the river rafting company, we give people of all backgrounds the
experience of a lifetime. With the help our of experienced guides, you will
learn rafting skills, enjoy scenic views, and have memories that will last a lifetime.

History

Since 1985, we have been provided the community with Rafting experiences
for all. What started off with three volunteers had now grown into a company
with 85 fulltime employees. Our hope is that our love of rafting is shared with
as many as possible.
