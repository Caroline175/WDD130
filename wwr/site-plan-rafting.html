Overview

Purpose

Our purpose for this website is to advertise our white water rafting company. This will show how people can make their rafting experience happen. It gives people the what, where, why, and how much.

Audience

The Audience for this site is those interested in White Water Rafting. Particularly those who don't know the area or are new to rafting.
