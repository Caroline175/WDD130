Plan Your Adventure

Ready for an exciting rafting experience? Contact us now!

Trip 1: Beginning River Adventure

This trip is friendly for all! We provide all the supplies you need. Our guides will be with you in the raft a take you for a 3 hour ride through the River Canyon.

Trip 2: Rapids Party

Enjoy an all day adventure on the water. Our guides will take you through all part of our park and teach you some rafting skills. This is our most family friendly adventure.

Trip 3: The Real Deal

If you want to challenge yourself, this is the trip for you! This 3 day adventure includes a day hike, 2 days of rafting, and 3 nights of camping. Our guides will teach you everything you need to know.
